Beaches & Relaxation

guanacaste

The beaches in Guanacaste are known for golden sands, surfing, and radiant sunshine almost all year. You'll find both luxury resorts and peaceful coves, especially near Santa Elena and the Gulf of Papagayo.

central and mid pacific

This zone mixes city energy and nature. Explore Puntarenas' pier, enjoy seafood, or head to Jaco and Manuel Antonio for hiking, monkeys, and surf-ready waves backed by tropical forest.

south pacific

Discover Golfo Dulce and the untouched Osa Peninsula. Coral reefs, dolphins, and deep jungle make this region perfect for wildlife lovers, with national parks like Corcovado offering unmatched biodiversity.

south caribbean

Puerto Viejo and Cahuita welcome you with Afro-Caribbean charm, colorful coral reefs, and black-and-white sand beaches. Food, music, and nature make this region unforgettable.
